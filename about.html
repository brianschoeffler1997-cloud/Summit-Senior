Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Brian Schoeffler

I started Summit Senior Services because I know firsthand how overwhelming this process can be. Whether a family is helping a parent downsize after decades in the same home, or navigating a move to assisted living, the emotional weight alone can feel like too much — before you've even thought about the logistics.

My passion is simple: I want to be the person families in Greater Cleveland can call and genuinely feel relieved afterward. Not more stressed. Not more confused. Relieved. That's the standard I hold myself to on every single job.

I believe the transition to a new chapter of life deserves patience, dignity, and a personal touch. You shouldn't have to coordinate five different vendors, chase down movers, or figure out what to do with a houseful of belongings on your own. That's exactly what I'm here for.

When you work with Summit Senior Services, you're not hiring a faceless company. You're working directly with me — someone who genuinely cares about your family's outcome and won't consider the job done until you're truly settled and at ease.

— Brian Schoeffler, Founder
