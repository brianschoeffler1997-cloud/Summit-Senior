Who We Are

Cleveland's Dedicated Senior Transition Specialists

At Summit Senior Services, we understand that transitioning a home isn't just a logistical task — it's one of life's most emotional moments. Whether you're a senior ready for your next chapter or an adult child helping a parent downsize, we're here to take the weight off your shoulders.


We handle senior moves and downsizing as one integrated service, right here in Greater Cleveland. No juggling multiple companies. No miscommunication. Just one trusted team from start to finish.

What We Do

Every Service You Need, Tailored to Your Situation

No two families are the same. We don't offer one-size-fits-all packages — instead, we sit down with you, understand your specific needs, and build a custom plan that fits your timeline, your budget, and your family.

Below is a full picture of what we can handle — use as much or as little as you need.

📦

Packing & Unpacking

Professional, careful packing of an entire home or specific rooms — and full unpacking and setup at the new location so it feels like home immediately.

🚚

Senior Move Coordination

We manage and oversee every aspect of moving day — scheduling, supervising movers, and making sure nothing gets lost or damaged in the process.

🗂️

Downsizing & Decluttering

We help you make thoughtful decisions about what to keep, donate, sell, or discard — at your pace, with zero pressure.

📐

Floor Plan & Space Planning

We help plan your new space before moving day so furniture fits perfectly and your new home is comfortable and functional from day one.

🎁

Donation & Disposal

We coordinate donations to local charities, arrange junk removal, and ensure unwanted items are handled responsibly so you don't have to lift a finger.

🏠

Home Cleanout

After a sale or move, we can arrange a full cleanout of the property — leaving it clean, empty, and ready for listing or handover.

🌿

Settling-In Support

We don't disappear after move-in. We hang pictures, connect electronics, organize closets, and make sure your loved one feels truly at home.

📱

Online Item Sales

Want to sell furniture, antiques, or household items without the hassle of a traditional estate sale? We photograph, list, and manage the sale of your items on Facebook Marketplace and other platforms — handling inquiries and pickups so you don't have to.

Not sure which services you need?

That's exactly what our free consultation is for. We'll come to you, assess the situation, and put together a clear, custom plan — with transparent pricing and no obligation.
